CLUB RULES

At UYB.FC we believe that when we play with respect, train with purpose, and support one another, we don’t just build better players — we build a stronger team and a proud community. Welcome to the journey. Play fair, play hard, and play with heart.

To ensure that every member, coach, and supporter enjoys a safe, fair, and positive environment, we have established a set of rules and regulations that guide our conduct and operations. These guidelines promote discipline, unity, and professionalism, helping us uphold the highest standards of integrity within the sport.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

Match Duration


Total duration is 60 minutes — divided into two halves of 30 minutes each, with a 5-minute halftime break.

Kick-offs


Kick-offs, goal kicks, and corner kicks follow standard FIFA rules. However, no offsides apply in 7-aside games unless agreed otherwise before kickoff.

Goalkeepers


Goalkeepers must stay within their penalty area. They can roll or pass the ball to restart play — no drop kicks or long punts beyond halfway (optional rule to encourage ground play).

Fouls


All free kicks are indirect unless the foul occurs inside the penalty area (then it’s a penalty). No slide tackling is permitted for safety reasons.

Penalties


Penalty spot is placed 8 yards (7.3 meters) from goal. Only the goalkeeper and penalty taker are allowed inside the area during the kick.

Scoring and Restarts


Goals can only be scored from the opponent’s half. After a goal, play restarts with a kick-off from the center.

Fair- Play


Yellow card = 2-minute suspension (“sin bin”). Two yellows or a red card = player sent off for the rest of the match (no replacement allowed).

Match Conduct


A designated match coordinator/referee ensures fair play and keeps time. Late arrival beyond 10 minutes may result in a default loss or reduced playtime.
